About

So why DevelopingDipen.com? And not DipenPatel.com?

I am career changer so not only am I learning Web Development, I’m also developing a new identity for myself—I’m Developing a new Dipen alongside Web Applications!

What did I do before Web Development?

Well, I wasn’t around before 1989 when World Wide Web was created, but I did work as a Health Care Professional before my decision to change careers. I worked with Physical Therapists to help provide treatments to 100’s of patients for 10’s of illnesses/injuries.

Why Web Development? Why Front-End Development?

I am a visual creative who enjoys creating with my hands, be it beautifully plated food, curated photography content or stylish men’s ensembles. And Web Development is defnity a creative endeavor. From my very first exposure to Web Development, I knew I wanted to focus on Front-End Development—while still learning and enjoying Back-end Development— because of its creative and visual nature.

Who am I?

I am a self-taught Front-End Web Developer with a passion for creative minimalism and simplicity when it comes to effective Web Design/Development. I am driven by the curiosity of learning, the passion for creating and the desire to master new skills. I believe sometimes less is more and like to employ this thought in my work.

In case you missed it, I also enjoy cooking, photography and men’s fashion. Want to know more? Shoot me an Email, let’s grab some coffee together!
